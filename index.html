Dog Shooter Game

Welcome to the village! You need to defeat the dogs that are terrorizing the area.

Find and defeat the boss dog to win the game!

  • WASD: Move around the village
  • Mouse: Look around and aim
  • Left Click: Shoot
  • R: Reload your weapon
  • E: Pick up power-ups and bandages
  • H: Use bandage to heal (2 hearts)

Watch out for the boss dog that appears after you explore the village!

Dogs will become aggressive if you get too close to them. Stay away until you're ready to fight!
